🎯 HOE GEBRUIK JE DIT SAMEN MET VSA?
Stap 1: Open Finviz of Stock Analysis vóór de markt opent (06:00–09:30 NL tijd).
Stap 2: Filter op premarket change ≥ +10% en prijs < $100. Noteer de top 5 symbolen.
Stap 3: Voer elk symbool in de VSA analyser hierboven in. Kijk of het signaal de move bevestigt.
Stap 4: Alleen instappen als de VSA bias BULLISH is én het checklist ≤ 1 rode stip heeft.

⚠️ Let op: Stocks die +30% of meer premarket stijgen zijn vaak al te laat om in te stappen — het risico op een snelle reversal is hoog. Focus op +10% tot +25% met bevestiging vanuit het chart.

When & how to use this tool
What time period it covers, and when to run a scan
▼
📅
What time period does a scan cover?
This tool does NOT just look at today's price. It looks back over the last 20–30 trading days (roughly 4–6 weeks). It reads each day like a chapter — the price, how much it moved, and how many people were trading. Then it spots the important moments hidden in that history.
YOUR TRADING DAY — STEP BY STEP
STEP 1
☀️ Before the market opens
Run a scan on any stock you're watching. This tells you the overall direction and any important signal zones from recent weeks. This is your game plan for the day.
STEP 2
✅ Check the bias first
Is it BULLISH, BEARISH or NEUTRAL? If NEUTRAL — consider sitting out. Only trade in the direction the bias points.
STEP 3
🔍 Read the signals
Look at the VSA signals. Are they mostly green or red? Do they match the bias? More signals agreeing = stronger edge.
STEP 4
📋 Check the checklist
Before placing on eToro, run through the dots. 2+ red dots = skip it. Wait for a cleaner setup.
💡 Best times to scan: Evening after US markets close, or morning before they open. Re-scan mid-week if a stock makes a big move — fresh signals appear. Avoid scanning during big news events — signals will be noisy.
